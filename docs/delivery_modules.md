## Dispatch & Delivery Management (Route & Fleet Module)

### 1. Route & Schedule Planning
- **Route creation** — Ability to create daily/weekly routes for each individual driver/vehicle.
- **Route optimization** — Automatically calculate the optimal route by factoring in traffic, distance, priority, and time-window constraints; GPS tracking helps drivers complete deliveries faster through route optimization.
- **Recurring vs. one-time schedules** — Pickups can be set as either one-time or recurring, with drivers able to view them on the mobile app.
- **Time-window / appointment slots** — Ability to schedule deliveries within a customer-requested window (e.g., 9–11 AM), as well as day-of-week constraints (delivering only on days the customer can accept delivery).

### 2. Order/Manifest Linking
- Delivery schedules are directly linked to orders (contract-based or on-demand) — when a schedule changes, the order status updates automatically.
- **Load sheet / packing report generation** — Automatically generate what needs to be delivered to which customer per route; track linen pickup and drop-off events, create packing reports for customers on each route, and edit delivery schedules as needed.
- **Delivery manifest / container ticket auto-generation** per route.

### 3. Driver/Fleet Assignment
- Assign routes to individual drivers; admins can assign linen pickups to drivers via the admin portal.
- Drivers can view their day-by-day schedule via the mobile app.
- Factor in vehicle/labor capacity constraints (working hours, breaks).

### 4. Real-time Tracking & Validation
- **GPS live tracking** — Real-time visibility into the driver's current location.
- **Pickup/delivery event logging** — Timestamp, cart count, and weight logged per event.
- **Load validation** — Before each delivery, drivers can validate carts or bags against a system-generated load sheet to ensure they have the correct items.
- **Proof of delivery** — At drop-off, drivers scan items and take a photo for documentation, ensuring the correct linen reaches the intended customer.

### 5. Communication & Notifications
- Automatic SMS/email notifications to customers (e.g., when linen leaves the factory, or when pickup is completed) — customers automatically receive SMS notifications when their linen leaves the plant, and system-generated SMS notifications when drivers pick up carts.
- Alerts to drivers for route changes or rush orders.
- Driver notes (daily or permanent instructions per stop).

### 6. Exception & Change Handling
- **Mid-route adjustments** — Ability to insert rush orders or last-minute pickups.
- **Change requests** — Adjust quantity/schedule based on customer requests; add new change requests on the customer's behalf, and adjust or update delivered linen quantities as required.
- **Failed/missed delivery handling** — Rescheduling workflow.

### 7. Reporting & Analytics
- Generate multiple key revenue reports based on delivery routes.
- Track delivery accuracy / on-time rate.
- Driver performance KPIs.
