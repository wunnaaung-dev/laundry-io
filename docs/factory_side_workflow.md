## Factory-Side Workflow (After Driver Delivery)

Once the driver delivers the items to the factory, the following workflow is carried out on the factory side:

- **Check-in & Verification:** Scan-in the orders sent from the hotel and confirm their arrival at the factory.
- **Sorting & LOT Creation:** Sort the received items by category and systematically group them using the LOT concept.
- **Processing Updates:** Update the status of each washing stage (Washing, Drying, Ironing, Folding) in the system in real time.
- **Warehouse Management:** Move completed, laundered items into the clean warehouse zone (Clean Linen Staging).
- **Load to Truck:** Retrieve items from the warehouse and systematically load them onto the truck for the next delivery (Scan & Load Verification).
